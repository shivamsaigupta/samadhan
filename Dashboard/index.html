Experience real problems immersively

Samadhan puts government officials in the shoes of the common man. Good solutions come from understand the problem first. Samadhan's network of VR film-makers use the Samadhan platform every month to create a VR experience for the top 2 issues of each area. They upload it to the Samadhan web server and the client app fetches the videos from the server.
